A 4 in 1 pizza has different toppings in each corner. This pizza is great for people who can't make up their minds, or for those afriad of commitment.

ingredients

  • Cooking spray

  • Corn meal

  • 1 can of refrigerated pizza crust

  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil

  • ½ cup (2 ounces) of shredded cheddar cheese

  • 1½ cups (6 ounces) of shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Barbecue Chicken Pizza Corner
  • 1 tablespoon chopped red onion

  • ½ cup BBQ sauce with shredded chicken

    • Pizza Margherita Corner
  • 1 tablespoon of shredded (fresh) Parmesan cheese

  • 4 or 5 ¼inch slices of Roma (aka plum) tomatoes

  • 1 tablespoon of thin strips of fresh basil

    • Hawaiian Pizza Corner
  • 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce

  • 2 slices of Canadian bacon, cut in quarters

  • ¼ cup of drained chopped pineapple

    • Pepperoni Pizza Corner
  • 2 tablespoons pizza sauce

  • 8 or 10 slices pepperoni

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F

  2. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray

  3. Sprinkle lightly with cornmeal, if desired

  4. Unroll pizza dough and press out on cookie sheet into a 12 by 14 inch shape

  5. Brush with olive oil

  6. Bake crust 6 or 8 minutes

  7. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese in a cross shape to divide crust in fourths

  8. In each corner, spread one of the above toppings (or one of your own!)

  9. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top of all

  10. Bake another 10 to 14 minutes,until cheese is bubbly but not burned

  11. Cut each corner into 4 squares  instructions src
