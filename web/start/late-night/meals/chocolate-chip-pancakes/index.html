A pancake is a flat cake, often thin, and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may also contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often with oil or butter. In Britain, pancakes are often unleavened, and resemble a crêpe. In North America, a raising agent is used (typically baking powder). The American pancake is similar to a Scotch pancake or drop scone. Commercially prepared pancake mixes are produced in some countries.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 2 Eggs

  • 3 Cups (750ml) buttermilk*

  • 3 Cups (750ml) all-purpose flour, sifted

  • 1 tsp (5ml) baking soda

  • 2 tsp (10ml) baking powder

  • 1 tsp (5ml) salt

  • 2 tbsp (30ml) sugar

  • 1/4 cup (60ml) melted butter

  • 1oz (20g) Chocolate Chips

    • * If buttermilk is not available in your area, add 1 tbsp (15ml) of lemon juice to 3 cups (750ml) of milk and let stand at room temperature for 10 to 15 minutes.

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs & the buttermilk in a large enough bowl

  2. Add the remaining ingredients except the chocolate chips and mix well

  3. Pour the mix into a hot pan or skillet (as large or as small as you'd like)

  4. Drop some chocolate chips while they are cooking

  5. Cook until golden brown, or until they are satisfactory to your liking
Estimated Calorie Count: 2496 total / ~ 416 - 624 per pancake

*This recipe serves 4 - 6
