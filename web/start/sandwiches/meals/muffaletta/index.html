A traditional style muffuletta sandwich consists of a muffuletta loaf split horizontally and covered with layers of marinated olive salad, mortadella, salami, mozzarella, ham, and provolone. The sandwich is sometimes heated to soften the provolone. Quarter, half, and full-sized muffulettas are sold. The signature olive salad consists of olives diced with the celery, cauliflower and carrot found in a jar of giardiniera, seasoned with oregano and garlic, covered in olive oil, and allowed to combine for at least 24 hours. A muffuletta is usually served cold, but many vendors will toast it.
Wikipedia

ingredients

    For Salad:

  • 1 cup (250ml) Finely Chopped Green Olive

  • 1 cup (250ml) Finely Chopped Black Olive

  • 1/2 cup (125ml) Extra Virgin Olive Oil

  • 1/2 cup (125ml) Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley

  • 1/2 cup Finely Chopped Capers

  • 2tsp (10ml) Finely Chopped Fresh Oregano, or 1tsp (5ml) Dried Oregano

  • 1/2 cup (125ml) Chopped Pimientos

  • 1/4 cup (60ml) Chopped Pickled Cocktail Onions

  • Salt & Freshly Ground Black Pepper

  • 1tbsp (15ml) Lemon Juice

    • For The Sandwich:

  • 1 Round Loaf of Italian or French Bread, ~8 - 9 inches (20 - 22cm) Diameter

  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce

  • 4oz (125g) Thinly Sliced Mortadella

  • 4oz (125g) Thinly Sliced Salami

  • 4oz (125g) Thinly Sliced Fontina, Provolone, or mozzarella Cheese

  • 1 Large Ripe Tomato - Cut Into Thin Slices
Use your favorite cold cuts in place of those we have suggested here if you prefer

instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients for the olive salad and mix well in a large bowl ( Cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours)

  2. Slice the bread in half horizontally and scoop out some of the soft bread inside

  3. Drain the olive salad, reserving the juice, and liberally brush the juice on each half of the loaf.

  4. Spread half the olive salad on the bottom half of the loaf

  5. Add the lettuce, meats, cheese, and tomato in layers

  6. Top with the remaining olive salad and cover with the top of the loaf

  7. Wrap tightly in plastic or paper and place on a large plate

  8. Place another plate on top, and weigh this down with some canned goods

  9. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour (max. 6) - cut into wedges to serve
Estimated Calorie Count: ~1253 (Sandwich Only) total / 208 ~ 313.3 per serving

*This recipe serves 4 - 6
