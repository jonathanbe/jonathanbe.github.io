Onion rings are a form of snack or side dish commonly found in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia, and some parts of Asia. They generally consist of a cross-sectional "ring" of onion (the circular structure of which lends itself well to this method of preparation) dipped in batter or bread crumbs and then deep fried; a variant is made with onion paste.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 1/2 in. pieces

  • Cornmeal as needed

  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice as needed

  • 3 tbsp Chipotle BBQ Rub

  • Oil for deep-frying

  • Black pepper

    • Chipotle BBQ Rub
  • 1 cup paprika
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup chipotle powder
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup dried rosemary
  • 1/4 cup dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup dehydrated lemon peel

    • Combine all ingredients and keep in airtight container - cool

instructions

  2. Place cornmeal in a pie pan and lemon juice in another pie pan

  3. Dip chicken pieces in lemon juice then season with Rub. Dip again in lemon juice

  4. Dredge pieces, three at a time, into cornmeal then dip in lemon juice again then dredge again in cornmeal

  5. Heat oil to 350° and fry pieces in batches for 6 minutes. Drain on a cooling rack but not paper towels. Sprinkle with black pepper and serve  instructions src
