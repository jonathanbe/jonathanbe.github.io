Mozzarella sticks are elongated pieces of battered or breaded mozzarella. Mozzarella sticks may be served with tomato or marinara sauce, as well as plum sauce, jalapeño jelly, barbecue sauce, honey mustard sauce, and ranch dressing. Cheeses other than mozzarella are also fried, including provolone, cheddar, and American.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • ¼ pound fresh mozzarella cheese

  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs

  • 1 large egg

instructions

    li>Preheat deep fryer to 350°F (175°C)

  1. Slice mozzarella into sticks 1-3 cm in diameter

  2. In a small bowl, whisk egg

  3. Fill another small bowl with bread crumbs

  4. Dip 3-4 sticks at a time into the egg

  5. Shake excess egg off sticks and place them in the bread crumbs

  6. Shake bowl of bread crumbs, evenly coating all sticks. Add more bread crumbs as needed

  7. Place coated sticks to the side and repeat for remaining sticks

  8. After all sticks have been coated, repeat the egg-dip and crumb-coat for each once more

  9. Place all sticks in fryer basket and submerge in 350°F oil

  10. Let sit for 30 seconds, gently rocking basket occasionally

  11. Lift basket and let cool for a few seconds. If cheese is not beginning to ooze out from half the sticks, re-submerge in oil for another 10-15 seconds, gently shaking the basket. Repeat until the cheese is just starting to ooze out

  12. Dump onto paper towel and pat off excess oil gently

  13. Serve hot  instructions src

    14. Mozzarella Bites Instructions
  14. Slice mozzarella into cubes 2-3 cm on a side

  15. Dip 5-6 bites at a time into the egg

  16. Continue with the rest of the recipe as above, and enjoy mozzarella bites
