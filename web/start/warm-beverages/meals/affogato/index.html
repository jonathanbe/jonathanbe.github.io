An affogato (Italian, "drowned") is a coffee-based dessert. It usually takes the form of a scoop of vanilla gelato or ice cream topped with a shot of hot espresso. Some variations also include a shot of amaretto or other liqueur. It should be noted that it is one drink and should not be considered coffee with ice cream.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice-cream

  • 1 tsp of coffee powder

  • 1 tsp of brandy

  • 1/2 tbsp of sugar

  • 1/2 tbsp cinnamon powder, for garnishing (optional)

instructions

  1. Place the ice-cream in a coffee cup

  2. Boil water in a pan, and then add coffee and sugar to it, and then mix well

  3. Add brandy

  4. Pour it over the ice-cream

  5. Serve immediately, garnished with cinnamon powder (optional)  instructions src
