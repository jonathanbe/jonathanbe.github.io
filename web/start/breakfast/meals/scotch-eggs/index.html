A Scotch egg consists of a hard-boiled egg wrapped in sausage meat, coated in bread crumbs and baked or deep-fried.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 12oz. (325g) Bulk Pork Sausage

  • 4 Eggs - Hard Boiled

  • 1 Egg - Beaten

  • Flour For Coating

  • Oil For Deep Frying

instructions

  1. Form the sausage meat around the hard boiled eggs in an even layer

  2. Dip in beaten egg

  3. Roll in flour

  4. Chill for 15 minutes so it firms up

  5. Deep fry, two at a time, in 325F (160C) oil, 5-6 minutes until golden brown all over

  6. Drain on absorbent paper

  7. Slice in half width-wise and serve hot with your choice of vegetables


*This recipeserves 4
