ingredients

  • 4 Strips of Bacon

  • 4 tbsp (60ml) of Olive Oil

  • 1 Cnion - Chopped

  • 1-2 Green Bell Peppers - Chopped

  • 2-4 Cloves of Garlic - Chopped

  • 1/4 tsp (1ml) Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)

  • 12oz (340g) Whole Bay Scallops, or Quartered Sea Scallops

  • 1lb (450g) Linguine

instructions

  1. Fry the bacon until suitable for your needs

  2. Cut into 1 inch (2cm) pieces

  3. Heat the olive oil over moderate heat.

  4. Saute the onion until tender but not brown, (~5 minutes)

  5. Add the bell pepper, garlic, and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring frequently, for 3 minutes

  6. Add the scallops & bacon - cook over high heat until heated through, (1 to 2 minutes) - Do not cover the scallops

  7. Toss the cooked pasta with the scallop mixture and serve immediately

*This recipe serves 4 - 6
