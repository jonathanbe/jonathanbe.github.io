Thai cuisine (อาหารไทย : Ar-Han-Thai) is one of the most popular cuisine[s] in the world. In 2011, seven of Thailand's popular dishes made it to the list of the "World's 50 Most Delicious Foods (Readers' Pick)"— a worldwide online poll of 35,000 people by CNN International. Thailand had more dishes on the list than any other country. They were tom yam goong (4th), pad Thai (5th), som tam (6th), massaman curry (10th), green curry (19th), Thai fried rice (24th) and moo nam tok (36th)
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 1 tbsp (15ml) of Soy Sauce

  • 1 tbsp (15ml) of Peanut Oil

  • 1 tbsp (15ml) of Lime Juice

  • 2 tsp (10ml) of Sugar

  • 1 tsp (5ml) of Hot Pepper Flakes

  • 1 Garlic Clove - Finely Chopped

  • Grated Zest of 1/2 Lime

  • 2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast Halves

  • Chopped Mint For Garnish

  • Chopped Cilantro For Garnish

instructions

  1. Whisk the soy sauce, oil, lime juice, sugar, red pepper flakes, garlic, lime zest, salt, and pepper together

  2. Add the chicken - marinate it, refrigerate for 1 - 2 hours

  3. Grill, broil, or saute the chicken breasts until done

  4. Serve garnished with mint and cilantro
Estimated Calorie Count: 785 total / 390 per serving

This recipe is serves 2
