Pad Thai, phat Thai or phad Thai is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food and at casual local eateries in Thailand. It is made with soaked dried rice noodles, which are stir-fried with eggs and chopped firm tofu, and flavored with tamarind pulp, fish sauce (nampla น้ำปลา), dried shrimp, garlic or shallots, red chili pepper and palm sugar, and served with lime wedges and often chopped roast peanuts. It may also contain other vegetables like bean sprouts, garlic chives, coriander leaves, pickled radishes or turnips (hua chaipo หัวไชโป๊), and raw banana flowers. It may also contain fresh shrimp, crab, squid, chicken or other proteins. Vegetarian versions may substitute soy sauce for the fish sauce and omit the shrimp.
Wikipedia

ingredients

  • 12oz (325g) Thin Rice Noodles

  • 3 tbsp (45ml) Peanut or Vegetable Oil

  • 3 - 4 Cloves Garlic - Finely Chopped

  • 1 cup (250ml) Peeled Shrimp - Roughly Chopped

  • 2 Eggs - Lightly Beaten

  • 3 tbsp (45ml) Fish Sauce*

  • 1 tbsp (15ml) Sugar

  • 1 - 2 cups (250 - 500ml) Mung Bean Sprouts

  • 2 Scallions (Spring Onions, Green & White Parts - Cut Into 1-inch (2cm) Pieces

  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup (60 - 125ml) Roasted Salted Peanuts - Finely Chopped

  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes

  • Chopped Fresh Cilantro (Coriander)

  • 2 - 3 Limes - Cut Into Wedges



    • *Available in finer supermarkets and Asian specialty shops.

instructions

  1. Soak the noodles in enough water to cover generously for 1 hour

  2. Drain thoroughly and toss with half the oil

  3. Heat the remaining oil in a wok or large, non-stick skillet over high heat until the oil is almost smoking

  4. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds

  5. Add the shrimp and cook 30 seconds

  6. Add the eggs and let sit for 10 to 15 seconds, until they begin to set

  7. Add the fish sauce, sugar, and the drained noodles and stir to combine

  8. Add the bean sprouts and green onion, reserving some of each for garnish, and stir to combine

  9. Transfer to a serving platter

  10. Garnish with the reserved bean sprouts and green onion, chopped peanuts, red pepper flakes, cilantro, and lime wedges
Estimated Calorie Count: ~1519 total / 253 ~ 379.75 per serving

This recipe serves 4 - 6
