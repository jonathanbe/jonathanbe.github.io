What is crumyum?


It is a program that will help will answer the question, 'what should I eat?' We've all been there, bored & hungry, with no idea on what you really want.

The whole process is pretty simple; start up crumyum when you're hungry, answer some questions, and we'll suggest some food for the mood you're in.

But crumyum isn't just for junk food. We have all sorts of recipes for all sorts of diets & food choices!
